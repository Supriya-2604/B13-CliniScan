About CliniScan Project

CliniScan AI is an advanced deep learning diagnostics platform meticulously designed to rapidly identify and localize pulmonary abnormalities from chest X-ray scans. Utilizing a fine-tuned ResNet50 architecture for robust pathology classification alongside a YOLOv8 network for precise object localization, this system serves as a powerful triaging toolkit for medical institutions. Our goal is to dramatically accelerate diagnostic turnaround times, reduce subjective errors, and enhance patient prioritization through completely automated, intelligent radiograph evaluation.
